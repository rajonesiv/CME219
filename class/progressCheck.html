Germany Trip Details

Butler University's College of Communication Study Aboard to Germany program provides students with a mix of media, culture, and language.

  • Taught by Butler instructors, local media experts, and guest speakers from news, radio/TV, concert industry, marketing, and government
  • Group field trips (to be determined) such as Deutshe Welle, ZDF, Neumann, Cologne Cathedral, Roman ruins, Babelsberg studio, and a concentration camp
  • Students and faculty stay together in hotels with easy public transpportation access to AIB and town
  • Students will produce video documentaries or interactive websites about a pre-approved topic related to the study abroad experience
  • Students will present their projects before our final group dinner

The cost of the study abroad experience is approximately $2977, not including airfare. Current airfare estimation is $1150 + tax.

Study Abroad Requirements

The College of Communication study abroad trip to Germany is open to all Butler students.

Priority will be given to upper-level College of Communication students

Enrollment is limited to 15 students.
